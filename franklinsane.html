Who is Sane?

The American Bowie.

He's been called "the American Bowie" — for the striking resemblance of his voice and his live performance style, reflecting one of the greatest rock stars of all time.

You haven't seen a real-live-pretend-to-be-Bowie till you've seen Franklin Sane. Growing up listening to the great Bowie albums cover to cover, Franklin developed an uncanny ability to sound like the real thing — and his training in classical voice, theatre, mime and dance all come to bear when he takes the stage as a complete apparition of the man who fell to earth.

"An immersive experience filled with charisma, style, soul."

As an original recording artist, Franklin's Bowie-flavored vocals as the voice of The Modern Clouds have hit #1 on Indie radio charts around the world.
